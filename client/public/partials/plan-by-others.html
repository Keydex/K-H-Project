PLAN BY OTHERS

Koch & Associates is a landscape construction company that welcomes the opportunity to work with plans designed by others.


We know that today’s homeowners have a variety of options in procuring landscape design services. While some enjoy having a single company perform both design and installation, others prefer to have their professional plans independently created prior to selecting a contractor.


Throughout the years, we’ve built solid relationships with quality landscape design professionals. We enjoy collaborating on projects and helping our mutual clients build highly personalized outdoor living spaces.
