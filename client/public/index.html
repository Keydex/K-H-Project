Koch & Associates, Inc. designs and builds high quality outdoor living spaces and landscapes, including out door kitchens, stonework, walls and patios, fire pits and outdoor entertainment areas, gardens, retaining walls and more. Our staff collaborates closely with homeowners and design professionals, ensuring that every element of the installation showcases the site's natural attributes while also reflecting the client's personal aesthetics. Our clients come from across the SF Bay Area including Campbell, Cupertino, East Palo Alto, Foster City, Fremont, Los Altos, Los Altos Hills, Los Gatos, Menlo Park, Milpitas, Mountain View, Morgan Hill, Newark, Palo Alto, Redwood City, San Jose, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Saratoga, Alameda County, Santa Clara County, and San Mateo County.
